Our Story

Davis Commercial Laundry Solutions has been serving Georgia and the Southeast for over 30 years. Founded by Mr. Davis, a lifelong entrepreneur and proud resident of Port Wentworth, we’re committed to helping laundromat owners succeed. Our experience spans equipment sales, installation, financing, and marketing – all tailored to the unique needs of each client.

As a family‑owned business, we value integrity, craftsmanship, and personalized service. When you work with us, you get more than machines – you gain a partner invested in your success.
